Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Data Collection

We collect personal information such as your full name, birth date, and government-issued IDs to verify your identity as a legitimate tenant or landlord. Financial data, including monthly income or allowance, is used solely for verification and property eligibility.

Data Usage
  • Verification of age (Must be 18+ for legal compliance).
  • Processing maintenance tickets and payment logs.
  • Communicating official broadcasts from landlords to tenants.
Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact our support team through the Contact Us page.
